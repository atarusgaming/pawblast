Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Drag the three pieces from the tray onto the 8×8 board. Fill a whole row or column to clear it and keep space open. There is no timer; the run ends only when none of your pieces fit.

What is Rescue Paw?

When you run out of moves, Draco offers to dig you out. The first rescue each run is free; after that you can watch an optional ad or spend treats. If an ad is unavailable, the rescue is granted free.

How do I remove ads?

Settings → Remove Ads ($4.99, one time). This removes banner and interstitial ads forever, and Rescue Paw becomes a free button.

I bought Remove Ads but still see ads, or I got a new phone.

Open Settings inside the app and tap Restore Purchases. Purchases are tied to your Apple Account and are never lost.

How do Daily Challenges work?

Every day there is one board, identical for every player. Beat the day's target score to keep your streak and stamp your calendar.

Is my data collected?

The game itself stores everything on your device. Ads are served by Google AdMob; see our Privacy Policy for details.
